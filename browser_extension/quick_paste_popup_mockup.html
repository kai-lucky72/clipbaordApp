How It Works:

When you press Ctrl+C (copy), the extension silently saves your copied content to the clipboard history without showing any notifications or popups.

When you press Ctrl+V (paste), a small popup appears near your cursor with your 5 most recent clipboard items. You can click any item to paste it, or just continue with the standard paste operation if you don't select anything.
